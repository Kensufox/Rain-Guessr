the rooms files, name based of the region it's from and the room name, stock data for the room: his name, coordinate, tile value, texture, and size

in this project, we only use the room name, coordinate, size and tile value

the name of the room are the region name, a letter and a number (CC_A02, 'CC' is the id of Chimeney Cannopy, 'A' mean the room is 1 screen, and '02' is the room id)

the tile value are number that refer to a type of element, it can be: wall, background, vertical or horizontal or crossing pole, pipe circuit, pipe entry, creature pipe/den.
